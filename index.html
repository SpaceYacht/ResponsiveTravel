Visit Starbase!

Visit the amazing SpaceX Starbase located in beautiful Boca Chica, Texas!

Location Details

The Starbase is located in Boca Chica which lies on the southern tip of Texas, on the Eastern portion of Cameron County (Soon to be Starbase, TX.)

Since 2014 Boca Chica has been home to a Starport owned and operated by Elon Musk's private space exploration company, SpaceX.

Boca Chica is also home to a Civil War battlefield: The Battle of Pamito Ranch, which some consider the final battle of the war.
However, this is boring and has nothing to do with space so best to stick with visiting the starport.
